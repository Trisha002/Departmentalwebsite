ABOUT OUR COLLEGE

Ramkrishna Mahato Government Engineering College, formerly Purulia Government Engineering College, is an engineering college located in Purulia, West Bengal, India. It offers undergraduate engineering degree courses affiliated to the Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology

Ramkrishna Mahato Government Engineering College, also known as Purulia Government Engineering College was set up in 2016 by the government of Bengal.
